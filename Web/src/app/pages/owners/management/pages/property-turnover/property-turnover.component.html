Property Turnover

Our Property Turnover Procedures consist of 3 equally important components.

PRE-LEASING INSPECTION

We perform a comprehensive Pre-Leasing Inspection prior to marketing your home. This inspection is designed to be more thorough and create a trackable action plan to get any necessary work completed. This process helps ensure that a new tenant's move-in goes smoothly and with no surprises.

  • Inspection reports can be viewed from online Owner Portal
  • Owners can easily approve any work to be performed
  • All work performed is tracked online
  • Properties are made rent-ready quicker, reducing leasing time
  • This systematic process increases tenant satisfaction

MOVE-IN INSPECTION

We don’t simply tick boxes on a list. Using our inspection software complete with multiple photos or video, our team performs an in-depth Move-In Inspection with the new tenant.

  • Completed Inspection reports are emailed to the Owner
  • Any work yet to be performed is communicated to the Owner
  • All data is stored and accessible in our Property Management software

MOVE-OUT INSPECTION

Upon the tenant’s move out, our team performs an in-depth inspection using our inspection software complete with multiple photos or video. This is done to compare the condition of the property to its move-in condition. In the unlikely event that any damage is done to the property requiring a claim against the security deposit, the property damage is thoroughly document through photos and video.

  • Completed Inspection reports are emailed to the Owner
  • Owners can easily approve any work to be performed
  • All work performed is tracked online
  • All data is stored and accessible in our Property Management software
